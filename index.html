This Project is Dedicated to My Dog, Dawson

She is currently 11 years old and wants a lot of yummy treats.
She first was taken in from a shelter, along with 4 of her children by a friend of mine in 2016.
One of her children was already an adult which my friend adopted.
The other 3 puppies had to be adopted one by one by puting advertisements on the internet.
All were adopted except Dawson since she was already 4 years old and nobody wanted her.
Since nobody wanted her she would have to go back to the shelter which she would be euthanized.
So, I adopted her and been with her since November 2016.
